"Bank CDS spread. This figure plots banks' CDS spreads over the TARP CPP investment horizon. Bank Index CDS Spread is measured by the DS North America Banks 5-Year Credit Default Swap Index. The dotted line labeled TARP Announcement corresponds to the TARP announcement on October 13, 2008. Initial TARP Investment is the date of the first TARP investment date on October 28, 2008. The dotted line labeled Original Maturity is the average earliest repayment date stipulated in the original TARP CPP Term Sheet. The dotted line labeled Early Repayment is the average TARP CPP repayment date conditional on repaying before the original maturity.",
